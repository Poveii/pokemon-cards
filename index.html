  • Charizard

    HP 300

    Description

    Generally, a Charizard is competitive. He loves battle and his competitive spirit is also notable, as he will never breathe fire at a weaker enemy unless provoked or ordered. When he gets too angry, the flames on his tail turn bluish-white.

  • Mewtwo

    Description

    Mewtwo was genetically created in a laboratory, being a clone of the mysterious ancient Pokemon Mew. It is a creature with a humanoid posture, but it has some feline characteristics.
